Homemade Pizza

Description

Homemade pizzas are a fast and easy meal to make after a long day at work. It will always ensure your are more than satisifed and is great for sharing with the family.

Ingredients

  • Aldi Garlic and Herb Pizza Bases
  • Aldi Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzerela Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Put sauce on pizza bases and spread with a spoon to cover the base.

  2. Cover pizza with shredded mozzerela cheese

  3. Add pepperoni slices (optional)

  4. Cook in over for 20 minutes
