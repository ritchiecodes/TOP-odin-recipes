Chicken Wraps

Description

Stop spending money on store-bought chicken wraps, and make these tender grilled chicken wraps instead! Theyâ€™re so easy and inexpensive to make, and the flavors are incredible!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Chilli Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Tortillas
  • Mozzerela
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Iceberg Lettuce

Steps

  1. Season the chicken cutlets

  2. Cook chicken on oiled pan

  3. Cut the chicken up and assemble the wraps

  4. Layer on the wrap, lettuce then chicken, shredded cheese and a drizzle of ranch dressing

  5. Seal tortilla and cook on grill for 2 minutes
